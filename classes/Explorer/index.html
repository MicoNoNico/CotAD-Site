  • Explorer

Chart the uncharted, mapping out the mysteries of the land with a keen eye for detail and a knack for getting out of sticky situations.

Abilities

Level 1+​

Trailblazer​

LevelDescription
1Slight increase in speed on natural paths and the ability to identify common safe routes.
2Improved efficiency in moving through difficult terrain such as forests or hills.
3Enhanced sense of direction, reducing the chances of getting lost and enabling basic map-making skills.
4Ability to notice and avoid natural hazards like quicksand, weak ice, or hidden pitfalls.
5Innate sense for detecting weather changes, allowing for better preparation and avoidance of natural elements.

Forager’s Eye​

LevelDescription
1You gain the ability to discern whether a plant is safe to consume.
2Your ability now gives you greater knowledge on specific plants, including its common name and additional properties.
3You can now identify the magical properties of plants, if any, and harness these properties for alchemical or magical uses.
4Your connection with plants allows you to interact with them, gaining insights, secrets of the land, or hidden resources.
5You gain the ability to synthesize new plant hybrids in your vicinity, combining properties of different plants to create unique flora with tailored benefits.

Level 5+​

Collector​

LevelDescription
1Gain the ability to reduce the perceived weight of an object for the user.
2Unlock a small pocket dimension capable of storing items. The weight limit is equal to what you can physically carry.
3Increase the effect of the weight reduction ability.
4Items stored within the pocket dimension are preserved in a stasis state, halting time for perishable goods.
5Increase the effect of the weight reduction ability.

Wanderer​

LevelDescription
1Increased endurance for longer treks and resilience to mild environmental discomforts.
2Faster recovery from minor scrapes, bruises, and fatigue.
3Improved ability to adapt to temperature changes, staying warm in the cold and cool in the heat.
4Mental clarity in stressful situations, aiding in escape or survival decision-making.
5Basic resistance to common natural toxins and pathogens encountered in the wild.

Level 10+

Animal Kinship

LevelDescription
1Reduced likelihood of wildlife perceiving you as a threat.
2Ability to interpret basic animal signals, aiding in the avoidance of danger or finding resources.
3Attract small wildlife, which can lead to discoveries or aid in foraging.
4Calm agitated animals, preventing potential attacks or conflicts.
5Basic communication with friendly or neutral wildlife, allowing for simple assistance or guidance.
